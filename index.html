Welcome to KIMS Taekwondo

TAEKWONDO is one of the most systematic and scientific Korean traditional martial arts, that teaches more than physical fighting skills. It is a discipline that shows ways of enhancing our spirit and life through training our body and mind. KIMS Taekwondo can help you to encrease your confidence, self control,respect,honor and integrity through our physical fitness classes. We are World Taekwondo Federation certified instructors. Our club have classes for: boys, girls, mens and womens, all of you are more than welcome.

The meaning of Taekwondo:
Tae means " to stike or brake with foot ",
Kwon means " to strike or brake with thefist ",
Do means " way , method or path".
So, Taekwondo meen " The way of the hand and the foot "or the way of kicking and punching.
